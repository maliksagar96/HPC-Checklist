Que 1 - What are the key features introduced in C++11?
Ans - a) auto keyword
    b) Range based for loop like
    for(auto& x:vec) {

    }
c) Replaced NULL with nullptr.
d) Lambda expressions were introduced.
e) Smart pointers were introduced.
f) move semantics + rvalue reference.
g) constexpr
h) static asserts
i) override and final keywords 
j) default and delete
k) std::thread
l) variadic templates were introduced.

Que 2 - What are the keywords auto and decltype?
Ans - Both keywords auto and decltype deduce the type at compile time. These keywords should be used when the datatype is too long std::vector<pair<int, int>> x.
